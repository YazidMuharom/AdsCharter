Project Charter

Business Advertising / Periklanan

Project Overview

This project charter outlines the inception and strategic framework for our Advertising Business. Our goal is to deliver innovative advertising solutions to enhance brand presence and maximize client engagement in the digital and traditional media landscape.

Objectives

  • Develop a comprehensive multi-channel advertising campaign platform.
  • Increase client brand awareness by at least 40% within one year.
  • Achieve measurable growth in target audience reach and engagement.
  • Establish partnerships with top media and influencer networks.

Scope

The project covers planning, design, and implementation of advertising campaigns across social media, TV, print, and digital platforms. It includes market research, creative development, media buying, and campaign analytics.

Key Stakeholders

  • Project Sponsor: CEO, PT Kreatif Media
  • Marketing Manager
  • Creative Team
  • Sales and Media Buying Team
  • External Advertising Partners

Timeline

  • Planning & Research: March 2024
  • Creative Development: April - May 2024
  • Implementation & Media Buying: June - August 2024
  • Monitoring & Evaluation: September - October 2024
